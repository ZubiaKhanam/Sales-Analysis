This graph shows the trend of sales varying over different months. The conclusion from this graph can be taken as the highest no. of orders were placed in the month of November

Sales and Profit Analysis by Customer Segment

sales_profit_by_segment = data.groupby('Segment').agg({'Sales': 'sum', 'Profit': 'sum'}).reset_index()

                     marker_color = color_palette[0]))

fig.show()

This graph shows the sales and profit between different customers
